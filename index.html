Steps to do Snake

Fold and unfold the paper in half both

Fold the top and bottom in to the centre along the dotted lines.

Fold the top and bottom in to the centre along the dotted lines.

Open the paper back up completely.

Fold the paper up along the dotted line.

Fold the paper down along the dotted line.

Fold the paper up along the dotted line.

Repeat steps 5-7 on the other side.

Fold the paper in half bringing the top part down and behind.

Your origami Snake is ready!
